About

DJ Igor Volya (Igor Miroshnychenko) is a Ukrainian DJ and music producer from Kharkiv. He has been performing since 2007 and creates energetic melodic techno and progressive music. His tracks have been played on radio stations across Ukraine and CIS countries, and one of his releases reached #1 in Kiss FM Top 20. He is also the author of an official remix with Amargalieva which gained more than 2 million streams. Music for night drives, clubs, DJ sets, festivals and long journeys.
